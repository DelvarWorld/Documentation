Getting Started

With the server running, open http://localhost:1337 in your browser to see the VorlonJS dashboard.

The last step is to enable VorlonJS, by adding this script tap to your app:

‹script src="http://localhost:1337/vorlon.js"›‹/script›

Now, when you open your app, you should see your client appear on the dashboard.

You can also open the sample page, which already has this script tag in it, to test your dashboard.
